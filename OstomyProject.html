Ostomy Appliance Modification

October 2023 • Biomedical Design • AutoCAD • 3D Printing

Summary

Redesigned a two-piece ostomy appliance interface to reduce skin pressure and leakage for a high-BMI patient by separating the pouch from direct skin contact while maintaining a reliable seal.

Skills & Tools

  • User-centred design
  • AutoCAD solid modelling
  • 3D printing & iteration
  • Team collaboration

Problem & Constraints

The patient experienced discomfort due to a flat flange design incompatible with abdominal geometry. Constraints included comfort, manufacturability, and seal reliability.

Design & Implementation

Developed a rigid conduit with an elastic sealing interface. Iterated CAD designs and produced low-fidelity 3D printed prototypes for evaluation.

Results & Learnings

Modular sealing improved comfort and testing efficiency. Learned the value of prioritizing user comfort over theoretical optimization in early designs.
